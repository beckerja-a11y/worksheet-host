Day 1: My Freedoms (Natural Rights)

The "Go-Free" Paper: The Declaration of Independence

Reading: The Declaration and My Freedoms

Long ago, we wrote a special paper called the **Declaration of Independence**. This paper was important because it said we wanted to be **free** from a big King's rules. This paper is the "Go-Free" Paper!

The paper says that every person is born with special gifts. These are gifts from nature, not from a government. We call these gifts **My Freedoms** (or Natural Rights).

What are **My Freedoms**? They are the right to **Live**, the right to be **Free**, and the right to feel **Happy**. No boss can take these basic freedoms away!
